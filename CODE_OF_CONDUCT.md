# Code of Conduct

## Our pledge

We want this project to be a welcoming place for everyone, whether you are shipping your first dataset or your hundredth. We pledge to make taking part a friendly and harassment free experience for all, regardless of age, body size, disability, ethnicity, gender identity and expression, level of experience, nationality, personal appearance, race, religion, or sexual identity and orientation.

## What we ask of you

Things that keep this a good place to be:

- Be kind and assume good faith.
- Welcome questions, including basic ones. Everyone starts somewhere.
- Give and accept feedback gracefully.
- Focus on what is best for the community and the people using the tools.

Things that are not welcome here:

- Harassment of any kind, public or private.
- Insults, personal attacks, or demeaning comments.
- Publishing someone else private information without their consent.
- Any conduct that a reasonable person would find inappropriate in a professional setting.

## Scope

This applies in all project spaces, including issues, pull requests, and discussions, and when someone is representing the project in public.

## Reporting

If you see or experience behavior that breaks this code, please report it by opening an issue, or by contacting the maintainer, Aivaras Navardauskas, on GitHub at A1VARA5. Reports will be reviewed and handled with discretion. The maintainer is responsible for clarifying standards and may remove contributions or contributors that do not follow this code.
